Industries Served

We Know Your Industry.
Not Just Your Equipment.

Foam quality failures look different in every industry. Our platform is tuned to the specific chemistry, cycle times, and quality standards of each market we serve — not a one-size-fits-all solution.

Automotive Seating

Tier suppliers cannot afford rework at automotive volumes. Our AI monitors shot weight, cream time, and demold consistency on every cycle — catching drift before it becomes a defective seat. Full per-part traceability supports IATF 16949 audits without extra paperwork.

Refrigeration & Appliances

Void-free cavity fill is the difference between passing and failing energy efficiency ratings. Our systems hold mix ratios and injection pressure to tight tolerances every cycle, while our AI flags any deviation in real time — so you're not discovering problems at final inspection.

Insulation Panels & Building Materials

High-volume PIR/PUR panel lines live and die by consistency. Our dispensing systems and AI monitoring lock in cream time and surface quality board-to-board, shift-to-shift — giving you the process stability to confidently scale production.

See It In Action

Real equipment. Real production lines. Running at customer facilities.

01

Process & Equipment Co-Optimization

Our AI learns the relationship between your machine parameters and your foam quality outcomes — then recommends adjustments that make equipment and process work in tighter harmony. The result: higher yield with less trial and error.

02

Predictive Fault Detection

By cross-correlating temperature, pressure, and flow data across thousands of shots, our AI spots the early warning signs of valve wear, filter clogging, or raw material inconsistency — before a single defective part is made.

03

Operator Intelligence & Guidance

Instead of staring at raw PLC numbers, operators see plain-language alerts and guided recommendations on a simple dashboard. Less expertise required to run the line well — which means lower labor costs and happier, more confident teams.

04

Full Shot-Level Traceability

Every injection cycle is timestamped and linked to mold ID, operator, material lot, and machine state. One-click audit reports for IATF 16949, customer quality reviews, or internal investigations — no manual log-keeping required.

05

Yield & OEE Analytics

Automated shift and weekly reports surface your real yield rate, top defect causes, and production losses in plain terms. Stop guessing where quality problems come from — the data tells you exactly where to focus.

06

Remote Support & Cloud Access

Our team monitors your machine telemetry remotely and can diagnose issues without a site visit. Deploy on a local industrial PC for air-gapped plants, or connect to the cloud for multi-site visibility — your choice.
