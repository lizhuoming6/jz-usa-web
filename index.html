Industries Served

Built for the Processes
That Demand Precision

Every foam application has unique chemistry, cycle times, and quality tolerances. Our systems are configured and tuned specifically for your industry.

🚗

Automotive Seating

From headrests to full seat cushions, our equipment handles flexible and semi-rigid formulations with the shot-weight consistency and cycle speed that automotive tier suppliers demand. Full traceability per VIN-compatible seat.

❄️

Refrigeration & Appliances

Cavity fill for refrigerators, freezers, and water heaters requires void-free, dimensionally stable rigid foam. Our systems deliver precise mix ratios and injection pressure to meet insulation performance specs every cycle.

🏗️

Insulation Panels & Building Materials

Continuous and batch panel lines for PIR / PUR rigid foam require consistent cream time and surface quality across high-volume runs. Our dispensing heads and data monitoring ensure board-to-board uniformity.

Smart Platform

From Machine Data
to Manufacturing Intelligence

Our IIoT platform connects directly to your foam machine's PLC, aggregates process parameters in real time, and surfaces insights through an intuitive web dashboard — no IT department required.

01

Real-Time Parameter Capture

Temperature, pressure, flow rate, and shot weight are logged at millisecond resolution for every injection cycle. Full parameter history — searchable, exportable, and audit-ready.

02

AI-Assisted Fault Diagnostics

Our rule engine cross-correlates pressure drop, flow deviation, and temperature variance to pinpoint root causes — valve wear, clogged filter, raw material inconsistency — before defects reach the end of the line.

03

Process Quality Traceability

Every shot is timestamped and linked to mold ID, operator, material lot, and machine state. One-click reports for customer audits, IATF 16949 compliance, or internal quality reviews.

04

Cloud or On-Premise Deployment

Deploy our platform on a local industrial PC for air-gapped environments, or connect to the cloud for multi-site visibility. Retrofit-compatible with existing machines — no hardware rip-and-replace needed.

05

Shift & OEE Reporting

Automated daily and weekly reports show production output, downtime breakdown, and quality yield by shift. Identify your top loss categories without combing through raw logs.

06

Remote Monitoring & Alerts

Get notified the moment a parameter drifts outside spec — by email, SMS, or dashboard alert. Our team can also access machine telemetry remotely to support troubleshooting without a site visit.
