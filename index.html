Industries Served

We Know Your Industry.
Not Just Your Equipment.

Foam quality failures look different in every industry. Our platform is tuned to the specific chemistry, cycle times, and quality standards of each market we serve.

Automotive Seating

Tier suppliers cannot afford rework at automotive volumes. Our AI monitors shot weight, cream time, and demold consistency on every cycle. Full per-part traceability supports IATF 16949 audits without extra paperwork.

Refrigeration & Appliances

Void-free cavity fill is the difference between passing and failing energy efficiency ratings. Our systems hold mix ratios and injection pressure to tight tolerances every cycle — no surprises at final inspection.

Insulation Panels & Building Materials

High-volume PIR/PUR panel lines live and die by consistency. Our dispensing systems and AI monitoring lock in cream time and surface quality board-to-board, shift-to-shift.
