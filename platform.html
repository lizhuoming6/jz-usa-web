AI Platform

Your machine runs.
Our AI watches.

We connect directly to your foam equipment and give it a brain — one that never takes a break, never misses a shot, and tells you exactly what's going wrong before you get a bad part.

What the AI Does

Three things. Done automatically.

👁️

It watches everything

Every time your machine fires a shot, the AI captures the data — temperature, pressure, flow, mix ratio — and stores it. Nothing gets missed, even at 3am.

⚡

It spots problems early

When a parameter starts drifting out of range, the AI flags it immediately — before it turns into a defective part. Most issues are caught within the same shift they start.

📋

It tells you what to do

Instead of just an alarm, the system gives your team a diagnosis — what's wrong, where to look, and what likely caused it. Less guessing, faster fixes.
